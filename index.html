About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Atmospheric Sciences with a passion for understanding tropical cyclones, the Intertropical Convergence Zone (ITCZ), and the complex dynamics that govern our climate system. My research combines numerical modelling, theoretical analysis and observational datasets to unravel the physical processes behind hurricanes and climate variability.
