Tropical Cyclone Genesis

Investigating the environmental conditions and atmospheric dynamics that lead to the formation of tropical cyclones in the Eastern North Pacific. This includes analyzing vorticity, moisture convergence, and vertical wind shear.

ITCZ Variability

Studying the seasonal and interannual variability of the Intertropical Convergence Zone (ITCZ) and its impact on global weather patterns. This work leverages satellite observations and reanalysis data to track shifts in the ITCZ.
